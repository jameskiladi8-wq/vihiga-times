Elsie Muhanda's Bodyguard Dies in Chavakali Crash—What Happened Moments Before Raises Questions

27 Apr 2026 5 min read By Vihiga Times Correspondent

What started as an ordinary day in Chavakali quickly turned into a moment of deep shock and sorrow that has left two counties mourning.

A tragic road accident claimed the life of Brian Mulinya, a long-serving and trusted bodyguard to Kakamega Woman Representative Elsie Muhanda. The incident, which occurred within the busy Chavakali area in Vihiga County, has sent waves of grief through both Kakamega and Vihiga communities.

Brian was not just a security officer assigned to duty. To many who knew him closely, he was a calm, reliable, and deeply respectful individual who carried himself with quiet professionalism. For years, he stood beside his employer through public events, political engagements, and personal moments that required both trust and loyalty.

That trust is what makes his sudden passing even harder to comprehend.

According to initial reports, the accident happened along a section of road that is often busy with both vehicles and pedestrians. Details surrounding the exact cause are still limited, but what remains clear is that the impact was fatal. The news spread quickly, drawing attention from colleagues, leaders, and residents who were left struggling to process the loss.

A Heartfelt Tribute

In a heartfelt message shared on social media, Elsie Muhanda expressed deep sorrow over the death of her bodyguard. She described Brian not only as a trusted officer but also as a close friend who had become part of her extended family over the years.

Her message reflected both grief and gratitude—gratitude for the years of service, loyalty, and protection Brian offered, and grief for a life that ended far too soon.

"He was not just a trusted officer but also a close friend to me and my family," she shared, adding that his loss leaves a painful gap that words cannot easily fill. She extended her condolences to his family, describing him as a truly remarkable man whose presence brought stability and trust wherever he served.

A Community in Mourning

As news of his death spread, messages of sympathy began pouring in from colleagues, friends, and members of the public who had encountered him during his service. Many remembered him as humble, respectful, and dedicated to his work.

In Chavakali, the scene of the accident has now become a place of reflection. For many residents, it is a reminder of how quickly life can change on the road—how a normal journey can turn into heartbreaking news in a matter of moments.

Brian's passing also raises broader concerns about road safety in busy town centers like Chavakali, where traffic, pedestrians, and vehicles often share narrow or high-risk sections of road. While investigations are expected to continue, the community is already left with grief and unanswered questions.

A Legacy of Loyalty

For the family, colleagues, and friends of Brian Mulinya, the days ahead will be filled with mourning and memories of a man who served with loyalty and quiet strength.

And for those who knew him even briefly, his death is a painful reminder that behind every uniform is a life, a story, and people who deeply care.

Rest in peace, Brian Mulinya.
