Local Artists to Watch in 2026

11 Feb 2026 4 min read By Grace Atieno

Vihiga's entertainment scene is buzzing with fresh talent, as emerging artists across various disciplines make their mark. From musicians to actors to visual artists, these creative individuals are bringing new energy and perspectives to the local arts landscape.

We highlight some of the most promising emerging artists to watch this year. Their work reflects the diversity of talent in our community and points to an exciting future for the arts in Vihiga.

Musical Talent

The music scene continues to produce exciting new voices. Several young musicians are gaining attention with fresh sounds that blend genres and push creative boundaries. Their music addresses themes ranging from love and relationships to social issues and personal growth.

One artist to watch is a singer-songwriter whose soulful vocals and thoughtful lyrics have earned her a growing following. Her acoustic performances at local venues have become must-attend events for music lovers.

Visual Arts

"Art is my way of expressing what words cannot say. Every piece tells a story."

Visual artists are using their work to explore themes of identity, culture, and social change. From painters to sculptors to digital artists, these creatives are finding new ways to express themselves and engage with audiences.

Local galleries and exhibition spaces are providing platforms for emerging artists to showcase their work and connect with collectors and art enthusiasts.

Performing Arts

Theater and dance are also experiencing growth, with young performers bringing fresh energy to these traditional art forms. Drama groups are producing original works that address contemporary issues while entertaining audiences.

Dance troupes are experimenting with fusion styles that combine traditional African dance with contemporary forms, creating visually stunning performances that captivate audiences.

Supporting Emerging Artists

Supporting emerging artists is essential for the continued growth of the arts scene. Attending performances, purchasing artwork, and sharing artists' work on social media are all ways to contribute to their success.

Mentorship programs are also playing a role in nurturing new talent, with established artists providing guidance and support to those just starting their careers.

The Future Looks Bright

With so much talent emerging across disciplines, the future of entertainment and culture in Vihiga looks promising. These artists are not only creating great work but also inspiring the next generation of creatives to pursue their passions.

Keep an eye on these rising stars – you're likely to be hearing much more from them in the coming years.
