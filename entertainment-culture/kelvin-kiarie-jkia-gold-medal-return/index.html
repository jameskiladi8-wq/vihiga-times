He Won Two Gold Medals for Kenya… But What Happened at JKIA Left Many Talking

8 May 2026 4 min read By Vihiga Times Correspondent

Kenyan skater Kelvin Kiarie has become the center of online conversation after returning home from Egypt with two gold medals, only to reportedly find a quiet reception waiting for him at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

The talented athlete had travelled to Cairo to represent Kenya in the African Skating Championships, where he delivered an impressive performance and proudly raised the country's flag on the continental stage.

But while many expected a hero's welcome after such a major achievement, reports claim that only his family members were present at the airport to receive him when he landed back in Kenya.

The moment quickly attracted attention online, with many Kenyans expressing disappointment over the alleged absence of sports officials or government representatives during the athlete's arrival.

The Struggle Behind the Glory

What touched many people even more was the story behind Kelvin's journey to Egypt.

According to reports shared online, the skater allegedly struggled to secure proper support before the competition and was forced to look for alternative financial help to avoid missing the championships. Claims later emerged that funds meant to support his travel and participation had allegedly been misused, leaving the athlete in uncertainty just before the event.

Despite the difficulties, Kelvin reportedly refused to give up.

In another emotional revelation, the athlete thanked a close longtime friend who allegedly sent him KSh38,000 within hours after learning about the financial challenge. The support reportedly helped him continue with preparations and travel plans that eventually led to his success in Cairo.

"This is not just about medals. It is a story of resilience, sacrifice, and determination."

A Hero's Welcome From Family

Now, many Kenyans are calling his victory more than just a sports achievement. To them, it is a story of resilience, sacrifice, and determination.

Shortly after arriving at JKIA, Kelvin shared a warm family photo alongside his wife and children. In the message accompanying the photo, he thanked his loved ones for standing by him throughout the journey and for welcoming him home.

The touching moment won the hearts of many social media users, with some praising him for balancing sports, family life, and responsibility despite the challenges he faced behind the scenes.

Questions About Athlete Support

Others, however, shifted their focus to the larger issue of how athletes are treated in the country, especially those in lesser-known sports.

Several Kenyans questioned why an athlete who had just brought international glory to the country appeared to return home without official recognition at the airport.

Many online users described Kelvin as a symbol of hidden talent in Kenya, saying his story proves that many young people can succeed if they receive proper support and encouragement.

As reactions continue to spread online, Kelvin Kiarie's journey is now inspiring conversations far beyond skating. For some, his story is not only about medals, but about persistence, family support, and the silent struggles many athletes face before the world finally notices them.

Congratulations, Kelvin Kiarie. Kenya is proud of you.
