Rashid Abdalla and Lulu Hassan's Heated Live TV Exchange Leaves Kenyans Divided

8 May 2026 5 min read By Vihiga Times Correspondent

Popular media couple Rashid Abdalla and Lulu Hassan are once again trending online after a heated but entertaining exchange during their live relationship segment on Citizen TV.

The two journalists, who are widely admired for their chemistry both on-screen and off-screen, found themselves on opposite sides of a discussion involving marriage, cheating, and financial responsibility in relationships.

The debate began during their after-news relationship segment when Rashid revealed that there was a message from a viewer that Lulu allegedly refused to read aloud on air.

According to Rashid, the message involved a woman who cheated on her husband after being married for 12 years because the man had become jobless. The situation became even more controversial after Rashid added that the woman allegedly cheated with her husband's close friend because she no longer viewed her husband as important after losing his source of income.

The unexpected topic immediately changed the mood in the studio.

A Studio Divided

As Rashid attempted to explain the message, Lulu openly disagreed with some of his views and appeared to sympathise with the woman in the story. She argued that financial struggles in relationships can sometimes affect emotional connection and responsibilities within a home.

In a statement that quickly spread across social media, Lulu questioned how a woman could continue giving energy and effort in a relationship where the man was no longer contributing financially.

Her remarks instantly sparked reactions online.

"This is the kind of debate that gets everyone talking. Two people who love each other, disagreeing passionately about something that affects so many homes."

Rashid Fires Back

Rashid, however, strongly defended the husband's side and insisted that cheating could never be justified, regardless of financial problems in the marriage. The discussion became more animated as both presenters passionately defended their opinions while viewers watched the exchange unfold live on television.

At one point, Rashid jokingly stated that if he ever became wealthy after being treated badly, he too would "show his energy elsewhere," a comment that further fuelled reactions online.

Kenyans React

While the exchange appeared playful at moments, many viewers admitted the tension between the two presenters felt surprisingly real, making the segment even more entertaining for audiences.

Soon after clips from the show surfaced online, Kenyans flooded social media with reactions, jokes, and personal opinions about relationships and marriage.

Some viewers sided with Lulu, arguing that financial stability plays a major role in relationships and family responsibilities. Others defended Rashid, saying cheating should never be excused under any circumstance.

A section of fans even joked that the couple might one day have a full argument live on television because of how passionately they debate relationship topics.

Not the First Time

This is not the first time the two media personalities have sparked online discussion. In previous segments, they also found themselves disagreeing over polygamy and the idea of men marrying second wives, with Lulu openly opposing the practice while Rashid defended why some men choose it.

Their latest exchange has now become another viral moment, proving once again why many Kenyans enjoy watching the celebrity couple tackle sensitive relationship issues in such an open and relatable way.
