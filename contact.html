Frequently Asked

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

You can submit a news tip through our contact form by selecting "News Tip" from the subject dropdown, or reach out to us directly via WhatsApp for quick submissions. Include as much detail as possible, including location, date, and any relevant photos or documents. Our editorial team reviews all tips promptly and may follow up for additional information.
Businesses and organizations can contact our advertising department through the contact form by selecting "Advertising" as the subject. We offer a variety of advertising options including banner placements, sponsored content, and newsletter features. Our team will provide you with our media kit and rate card within 24 hours of your inquiry.
