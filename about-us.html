About Vihiga Times

Your trusted source for local and national news, delivering accurate, timely, and relevant information to the people of Vihiga County and beyond.

Our Story

Vihiga Times was founded with a simple mission: to provide the people of Vihiga County with accurate, timely, and relevant news that matters to their daily lives. What started as a small community initiative has grown into one of the most trusted news sources in the region.

We believe that informed citizens are empowered citizens. That's why we cover everything from local government decisions and community events to national policies and global trends that affect our readers.

Our Mission

To be the most reliable and accessible news platform for the people of Vihiga County, delivering quality journalism that informs, educates, and empowers our community.

Our Values

Accuracy: We verify every fact before publication. Our readers trust us to get it right, and we take that responsibility seriously.

Integrity: We maintain editorial independence and disclose any potential conflicts of interest. Our journalism is free from undue influence.

Community: We are part of the community we serve. We listen to our readers and reflect their concerns, aspirations, and achievements.

Innovation: We embrace new technologies and storytelling formats to better serve our audience in the digital age.

What We Cover

From breaking news and in-depth investigations to lifestyle features and entertainment updates, Vihiga Times covers the full spectrum of news and information that matters to our readers. Our dedicated team of journalists and contributors work tirelessly to bring you:

  • Breaking local and national news
  • In-depth investigative reporting
  • Business and economic analysis
  • Entertainment and cultural coverage
  • Sports and community events
  • Youth-focused content and opportunities
