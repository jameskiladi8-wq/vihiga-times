Our Story

The idea for Vihiga Times began in June 2025. The vision was simple but powerful: to build a trusted digital home for the people of Vihiga County.

The first public version was published on 18 March 2026, and Vihiga Times officially launched on 17 May 2026. From the very beginning, the goal has been to inform, educate, preserve culture, and celebrate the people of Vihiga County.

June 2025 — The vision for Vihiga Times was born.
18 March 2026 — First public version published.
17 May 2026 — Official launch of Vihiga Times.

Vihiga Times is more than a news website. It is a digital record of the county's history, traditions, achievements, businesses, schools, churches, fundraising events, youth, sports, and everyday life. The mission is to make sure every important local story has a place to be remembered.

Every community deserves a trusted voice. Vihiga Times is ours.

Our Mission

  • Deliver accurate local news that our community can trust.
  • Keep the community informed about what matters most.
  • Preserve local history and culture for future generations.
  • Support local businesses and help them grow.
  • Give every resident a voice in our shared story.
  • Celebrate achievements and promote development across Vihiga County.
  • Connect people through trustworthy journalism that brings us together.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted digital news and community platform in Vihiga County. We want to be the first place people turn to when they need to know what is happening in their community.

While we grow, we remain focused on what matters most: local people and local stories. We believe that every neighbourhood, every school, every church, every business, and every family in Vihiga County has a story worth telling.

We envision a future where Vihiga Times is not just a news source, but a living digital archive of our county's journey — a place where the past is preserved, the present is documented, and the future is shaped by an informed and connected community.

The Values That Guide Us

These principles are the foundation of everything we do at Vihiga Times. They shape our decisions, our stories, and our relationship with you.

Radical Accuracy

Every story is carefully checked before publication. Facts come before speed. If mistakes happen, we correct them openly and honestly. Our readers deserve nothing less than the truth.

Community First

Every decision starts with one question: "What is best for our community?" The needs of the people always come first. We are here to serve, not to exploit.

Accessible Truth

We do not spread rumours, misinformation, hate speech, or gossip. Every important story deserves fairness. Every person deserves to be heard. Both sides are considered before publication whenever possible.

Youth Amplification

Young people are the future of Vihiga County. We give special attention to education, innovation, talent, employment, sports, creativity, leadership, and youth success stories.

Integrity

Honesty, fairness, transparency, and accountability guide every story we publish. We hold ourselves to the highest standards because our community deserves nothing less.

Respect

Every individual, family, organization, religion, and culture is treated with dignity and respect. We believe in building bridges, not walls, within our community.

Founder • Owner • Developer • Editor • Publisher • Reporter • Website Manager

James Kiladi created Vihiga Times from a deep passion for technology, storytelling, community development, and preserving the rich history and culture of Vihiga County.

He personally manages the website, develops its features, writes and edits stories, oversees publishing, and continues improving the platform every single day. What started as a vision has become a living platform that serves thousands of readers across the county.

While Vihiga Times has grown tremendously since its launch, James believes the journey is just beginning. He is committed to working alongside the community to make the platform even stronger, more useful, and more impactful for everyone in Vihiga County.
