How It Works

Getting your advertisement on Vihiga Times is simple and transparent. No payment required before approval.

1

Complete the Form

Fill in your business details, upload your advertisement image, and select your preferred package.

2

Submit Request

Send your advertisement request to our team for review. No payment is required at this stage.

3

Team Review

Our team reviews your advertisement to ensure it meets our community advertising guidelines.

4

Approval & Payment

Once approved, we contact you with payment instructions. After confirmation, your ad goes live.

No payment is required before your advertisement has been reviewed and approved. This protects both the advertiser and Vihiga Times.
